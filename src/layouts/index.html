Preparing for the future

Technologies come and go but we believe DevOps working practices are the future.

Rather than hiring "DevOps Engineers" we prefer to focus on the underlying disciplines that undertake the working practices, participate in the community, and practice the culture. This helps our clients focus on the skills they need and the roles they need to fill.

Product Development

They'll lead the charge creating your products. Within DevOps, your software developers work with your Release Engineers and SREs in order to produce reliable and secure software.

Release Engineering

Experts in DevOps working practices that will help your teams build Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment pipelines that'll ensure your code is secure and well tested.

Site Relability Engineering

They lead the charge for ensuring your products are reliable and secure in production. They'll create environments and run tooling that'll give you that assurance you need.
